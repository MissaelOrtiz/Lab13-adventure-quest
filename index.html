You are a hero traveling to the cursed lands of Barheim...

You must help the locals with their problems, be that mischevious fey or dangerous monsters. Fight and collect enough gold to pay your way out of this or steel your resolve and grow your strength to take on evil head on. Fight roaming monsters for gold, pick your boons wisely, and adventure in the cursed lands of Barheim!

GOAL: Reach 12 ATK or 50 Gold to win! But beware, these lands are dangerous and death lurks around every corner.
